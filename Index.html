Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lead services

How are your leads generated?

Our leads come from multiple high-intent sources including specialized trading platforms, financial education websites, and targeted advertising campaigns. All leads are verified and have expressed specific interest in Forex or Crypto trading opportunities.

What targeting options do you offer?

We offer advanced targeting options including geographic location, trading experience level, investment size preference, trading platform familiarity, and specific asset preferences (Forex pairs, cryptocurrencies, etc.).

How quickly will I receive leads after signing up?

Once your account is set up and targeting preferences are configured, you'll start receiving leads immediately. For most clients, the setup process takes less than 24 hours.

What is your lead quality guarantee?

We guarantee that all leads meet our strict quality standards. If any lead doesn't meet our verification criteria, we replace it at no additional cost. Our average client sees a 15-25% conversion rate from lead to funded account.

How do I order leads?

You can place orders directly through our Telegram channel or via WhatsApp. We offer flexible payment options including cryptocurrency and wire transfer. Contact us using any of the methods above to get started.
